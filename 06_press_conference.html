Good morning, everyone. My name is Detective McGee.

On the morning of December 22nd, a man was found unconscious in his car.
The car was parked at the Riverside Parking Lot, Floor Level, in Boston.
The victim is Mr Bernard Smith, a 32-year-old lawyer from Boston.
He was found by a parking lot attendant at 6 AM.
The police arrived at the scene shortly after.
